            F.A.Q

The v-container component simply centers your content in the middle of the screen. If you add the fluid prop, your content will occupy the full width of the screen.

v-row (v-layout in v1.0) is used to group content and align it.

v-col (v-flex in v1.0) is a content holder that must be a direct child of v-row

Color (link

The dark prop turns the text white. This is also done in a color or class prop with white--text.

Background colors (color="red") are varied with darken-4 or lighten-5; text color is varied with text-darken-3 or text--lighten-3. accent-1 through accent-4 increase saturation for more vivid colors.

Tabs

The component also exposes a two-way binding property for controlling the currently visible tab — the v-model=”tabs” defines this. The variable tabs will hold a zero-based index of the selected tab.
